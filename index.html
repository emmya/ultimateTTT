how to play

this is a more sophisticated version of the classic tic tac toe game. in all 9 squares of a standard board, a smaller tic tac toe board is drawn.

each turn, you mark one of the small squares.

when you get three in a row on a small board, you've won that board.

to win the game, you need to win three small boards in a row.

here's the catch: you don't get to pick which of the nine boards to play on. an active board is determined by your opponent's previous move. for example, if your opponent plays in a lower right square...

...your next move must be in the lower right board.

if your opponent sends you to a board that's already been won or tied, you get to play wherever you like.

Game functionality was developed from scratch in JavaScript.

Animation was designed using combinations of CSS3, JQuery, and Transit.

The background and spaceship gif were created in Photoshop.
